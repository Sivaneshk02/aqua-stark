Research

  2. Projects: Detailed information on ongoing and completed projects.

We focus on investigating a wide range of marine ecosystems, from coral reefs to deep-sea environments.
Our drones collect vital data on marine species diversity, behavior, and population trends. Our research is divided into several key projects: Coral Reef Monitoring: Studying the impact of climate change on coral species and their ecosystems. Deep-Sea Exploration: Documenting species diversity in unexplored oceanic depths. Fish Population Studies: Tracking fish populations to analyze ecosystem health and sustainability. In collaboration with leading marine research institutes, we use this data to drive both scientific discovery and practical conservation measures.
