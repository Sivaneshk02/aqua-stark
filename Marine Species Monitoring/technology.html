Technology

Our underwater drone technology combines innovation and practicality to offer a comprehensive solution for marine species identification. Drones: Our fleet of drones is equipped with high-resolution cameras, sonar, and various environmental sensors. They can operate autonomously or be remotely controlled, capturing data from challenging environments. Artificial Intelligence: AI plays a crucial role in processing the large amounts of data collected. By identifying species based on their physical features and behavior, AI helps streamline the data analysis process. Live Monitoring: Our drones provide live data feeds, enabling researchers to monitor marine species in real-time. This allows for immediate identification and timely interventions when necessary. Data Security: We ensure that all data collected is securely stored and protected, while still promoting data sharing within the scientific community. With this technology, we hope to create a detailed and constantly evolving map of marine biodiversity that will help scientists better understand and protect the ocean's ecosystems.
